Instructions:

1. Select a Poisonous Flower (this is your game piece)

2. Select an opposing Poisonous Flower (this is your opponent)

3. All Poisonous Flowers have "health points" associated with them.

4. Click the ATTACK button to arbitrarily deplete your opposing Poisonous Flower's health points.

5. As soon as you click ATTACK, the opposing Poisonous Flower will issue a counter attack.

6. Objective: The first Poisonous Flower with zero health points loses.

7. If you win, select another Poisonus Flower to defeat.
